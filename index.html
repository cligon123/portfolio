Cheyenne Ligon

Cheyenne Ligon is a freelance journalist and graduate student at the CUNY Newmark Graduate School of Journalism.

Cheyenne Ligon is a freelance journalist covering immersive theatre, politics, travel, and anything else she finds interesting.

After graduating from CUNY, she hopes to make immersive virtual reality documentaries.

To get in touch with her, send her an email at cheyenne.ligon59@journalism.cuny.edu

Cheyenne has covered immersive theatre for the online publication No Proscenium since 2018. As a NYC-based correspondent, she has attended and written reviews for many of the city's hottest immersive experiences, including traditional immersive theatre, LARPs, megagames, and escape rooms.

Cheyenne is also a published travel writer, and loves fusing her passion for writing with her love of travel. From niche events in New York to off-the-beaten-path tourist attractions all over the world, Cheyenne writes about it all.

Since she began studying at CUNY, Cheyenne covers local politics in Flushing, Queens. Her writing focuses on housing, food insecurity, poverty, gentrification, and local politics.
